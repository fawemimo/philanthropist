Ademola Aladekomo

Ademola started CHAMS in 1985 as the first indigenous computer maintenance company in Nigeria

Early Career


Sir Demola Ben Aladekomo was born on December 31st, 1957 in Lagos state, Nigeria. He is a Nigerian technology expert, entrepreneur, philanthropist and Managing Director of Chams Plc. The young Ademola graduated from the University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University) in 1982 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. In 1984, he obtained an MBA from the University of Lagos. He was part of the first Chief Executive Program at the Lagos Business School (LBS) in 1991-1992, and an alumnus of the Lagos Business School.

Ademola Aladekomo Awards


1. Ademola is a Fellow of the Nigeria Computer Society (FNCS).
2. He is also a Fello of the Computer Professional Registration Council (CPN).
3. He is a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE).

EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATIONS

Sir Aladekomo graduated from the University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University) in 1982 with a degree in Computer Engineering, followed by an Master of Business Administration from the University of Lagos in 1984. Chams Plc is also the parent company of ChamsSwitch Limited, and ChamsConsortium Limited, and has substantial interest in ChamsMobile. He later founded DATA Foundation which has repeatedly awarded scholarships to students in public schools, while Volunteer Corps continues to bring alive his vision of using professionals as volunteers teachers to train students in order to prepare them for university and polytechnic education. Other non-profits Council Member, Osun State University.
