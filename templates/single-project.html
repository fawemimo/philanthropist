History of Most Rev'd Oliver Ali Aba (Jp)

Oliver Ali Aba was born on 27th May, 1960 to the family of Late Mr. /Mrs. Audu Aba, both of Ogbadibo Olachagbaha in Ogbadibo Local Government Area of Benue State.

ACHIEVEMENTS


Archbishop Oliver has always left legacy and memories of his great impact were ever he served. The following are among his numerous achievements in Ministry:
-Building of a standard bishop’s manse at the Diocese of Apa.
-Bringing electricity to the bishop quarters at the Diocese of Enone
-Building of a standard bishop’s court at the Diocese of Enone
-Fencing of the entire Bishop court at the Diocese of Enone
-Construction of accessible road network through the partnership of PW construction company at the Diocese of Enone
-Building of a duplex to house the Archbishopat Otukpo, Benue Archdiocese.
-Flooring of the entire Archbishop's court in Otukpo with cement blocks.
-Building of the a standard Prelate’s guest house within six weeks at the Archbishop's quarters in Benue
-Building of a standard chapel at the Archbishop's court in Benue
-Fencing of the entire Archbishop's quarters at Jesus College, Otukpo among others.

SPIRITUAL ACTIVITIES


Archbishop Oliver is committed to the spiritual development of the Church. He introduced monthly cooperate fasting and praying for the Archdiocese on every first Saturday of the Month in addition to other missions and evangelistic programs in the Archdiocese.
God bless the Prelate Elect.
God bless Methodist Church Nigeria.

EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATION

Oliver Ali Aba attended L.G.E.A Primary School, Okpefie in Okpokwu LGA, Benue State between 1970 - 1977 (FSLC) He proceeded to Lagos Secondary Commercial Academy, Obaniko, Lukpeju, Lagos State between 1977 - 1980, Government College, Otukpa, Benue State from 1980 - 1982 where he obtain his S.S.C.E. In his quest and passion to serve God, Oliver proceeded to St. Augustine Major Seminary, Jos between 1983 - 1986 where he got training in Philosophy, then Immanuel College of Theology, Ibadan 1987 - 1990 where obtained Diploma in Theology. He further went to the prestigious University of Ibadan between 1990 - 1992 where he bagged B.A. in Christian Religion Studies. He further went back to University of Ibadan between 1997 - 1998 to obtain M.A in Church History.

  • WORK EXPERIENCES

    Between 1992 - 1993 Oliver Aba did his compulsory NYSC in Government Collage, Kubwa, Abuja. He was posted to Methodist Church Nigeria, Kubwa between 1993 - 1994 to serve as Priest Between 1994 - 1995, Rev. Oliver served as the then Prelate Chaplain. He was returned back serve as Priest in Methodist Church Nigeria, Nyaya Group of Churches between 1994 - 1995 . In 1995 Rev. Oliver was posted to Methodist Theological Institute, Sagamu to serve as Lecturer. The post he served until 1998 when he was further transferred to head Hassan Odukale Methodist Theological Institute, Zonkwa in 1998 as the Rector. In 2002 Very Rev. Oliver was Elected Bishop and was translated to Methodist Church Nigeria, Apa Diocese as the Pioneer Diocesan Bishop. In 2007 Rt. Rev'd Oliver was translated to Methodist Church Nigeria, Enone Diocese as Diocesan Bishop. In 2013 he was elected an Archbishop Methodist Church Nigeria, Archdiocese of Benue. The position he held until his emergence as Prelate Elect during the just concluded Conference of the Methodist Church Nigeria, Hosted by BENUE Archdiocese but held in Abuja between 9th -14th August, 2022.

  • Post Of Responsibilities Held

    Archbishop Oliver is Chairman of Methodist Board of Theological College from 2012 - Till date – He is also a Member of Benue Pilgrimage Board, Makurdi, Benue State..
