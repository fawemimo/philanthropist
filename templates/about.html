ABOUT PHILANTHROPIST.

The entire globe is blessed with few individuals who often make active and unflinching efforts to freely promote human welfare by practically being involved in philanthropism. My interest to immortalize philanthropists is because they choose to gladly donate and bequeath significant portions of their wealth to help orphans, impoverished and sick people in the global community. Most of these philanthropists personally enroll and pay for the school fees of indigent children in different places across the globe. In times of hardship and distress, it is an indisputable fact that philanthropists reduce poverty rate and concurrently provide succor to the victims of natural and unforeseen circumstances like Earthquake, landslide, windstorm, epidemic, war, etc.. In addition, they fund medical and social research developments which can lead to the discovery of permanent treatments to pandemic diseases (e.g. Malaria) and solutions to social problems (like unemployment). Philanthropists also collaborate with governments and Non-governmental agencies to alleviate poverty globally.

ALADEOKOMO Wuraola Olamide is a founder of "philanthropists dot com". She believes that the charitable gesture of philanthropists in the world must be digitally recorded in digital book of honour so that their uncommon kindness can span and outlive several generations. What else should we offer a group of few citizens that love all humankind in the world! Theyre never tired of rendering humanitarian services to the human race despite the fact that the globe or individuals may not celebrate them. This project intends to digitally furnish global community with the records of notable philanthropists with the aim of enabling other people to learn and emulate them across the globe.

About the Researcher?

Aladeokomo Wuraola

ALADEOKOMO Wuraola Olamide is a final year Computer Science student in Wesley University, Ondo State. She designed “philanthropists dot com" as her final year project with the view to digitally keep the track records of the greatest philanthropists in the history of the world. She implemented her design with 12 philanthropists that are selected in Nigeria and around the globe. Finally, as the founder of the philanthropists dot com", the research scope and the functionalities of the design would be routinely extended in due course.

Philanthropist

It's important to note that philanthropy is not solely about monetary donations. Many philanthropists also engage in strategic philanthropy, where they use their expertise and networks to drive social change and address root causes of issues. This can involve advocacy, policy work, capacity building, and collaboration with other stakeholders.
